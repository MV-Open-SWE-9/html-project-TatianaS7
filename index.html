My Experience

Before I became a Software Engineer, I started with User Experience Design. In 2021, I took a 6-month course through eCornell, where I learned all the essentials of UXD, from ideation to prototyping. Prior to knowing what UX Design was though, I had ideas for webistes and apps, but I was coming from a business background so I knew it wasnt feasible to start up all of these companies. What I ended up noticing was that I put a lot of focus on what the digital aspect would entail rather than the logistics as a business. This is what led me to look more into creative career paths, even considering product design. I knew I was good at planning out digital applications and I wanted to keep doing it. One day I came across a TikTok about being a UX Designer and it caught my eye, ultimately leading to me taking the course.

Once I finished my first passion project, I realized I wanted to be able to actually build it out, so I branched off to the more technical side, learning JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL, Node.js, etc. This journey has been extremely fulfilling for me, just being able to develop these skills and see how far I've come. It's so exciting to see how I can incorporate my creativity into technology, and I look forward to learning more and contributing my knowledge to meaningful projects.
